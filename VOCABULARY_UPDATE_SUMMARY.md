## üåü Key California-Specific Features

### 1. **The "Context, Clue, Connect" Method**
Teaching strategy for word analysis:
- **Context**: Read the word in a full sentence
- **Clue**: Look for prefixes, suffixes, or roots you recognize
- **Connect**: Use the word in a sentence about your own life

### 2. **Focus on Nuances**
Extensive coverage of subtle differences in meaning:
- "Youthful" vs. "Childish" (directly from CA examples)
- Multiple word sets showing positive/neutral/negative connotations
- Understanding how word choice affects meaning
